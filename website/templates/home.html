Welcome to the Flip Card App

This app will help you study and recite. You can use the Flip Card App for vocabulary, definitions, quizzes and so much more.

If you head over to the "Study" page, you'll find all of your created cards. If this is the first time using this app or you've just created your account, then there aren't any cards visible.

To create a card, you can click on the "Create Card" page. There you can create an card you like. Be sure to confirm your card by clicking on the "save" button. Or discard it by clicking on the "discard" button.

How Do I Study?

Go over to the "Study" page and click on one of your created cards. This will turn the card around revealing the answer.

Have fun studying!
